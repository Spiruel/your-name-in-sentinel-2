Your Name in Sentinel-2

About

Type your name and see it spelled out using Sentinel-2 satellite imagery — real Earth features that happen to look like letters.

Satellite images are captured by ESA's Copernicus Sentinel-2 mission at 10 m resolution.

Using the app

  • Click a letter to open it in Copernicus Browser
  • Hover to see location info
  • Download saves your name as a PNG
  • Share copies a URL you can send to anyone

Live tiles

Open Settings and enter your free Copernicus Data Space OAuth credentials to load live Sentinel-2 imagery with configurable bands and dates.

Title of image —

Satellite imagery from ESA's Copernicus Sentinel-2 mission.

Share your name

Scan the QR code or copy the link to share your Sentinel-2 name.

The satellite images used here are part of ESA's Copernicus Sentinel-2 mission, providing global coverage at 10 m resolution since 2015.

Sentinel-2 is operated by the European Space Agency (ESA) under the EU Copernicus programme.
